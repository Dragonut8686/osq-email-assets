Multipurpose Packaging Solutions

The OSQ BOWL LINE is our most advanced round-shaped packaging. Its special design makes it ideal for storing greasy or liquid foods. Built to withstand any challenge, the dense cardboard combined with a high-quality barrier layer provides reliable protection against leakage.

The OSQ BOWL is ideal for:

✓ Grab and go snacks without heating (vegetable and lunch meat, nuts, berries, superfoods)
✓ Dairy products (ice cream, cottage cheese, yogurt)
✓ Hot dishes (soups, porridges)
✓ Desserts
