OSQ TABOX 440

New square format

An addition to the legendary range

OSQ TABOX 440 is a new format designed to highlight the shape of your product and is ideal for small square and round dishes.

It is a solution for restaurants, delivery services, retail, confectionery and businesses specializing in handmade products, where packaging is needed not only for protection but also for impression: your product looks like a carefully prepared gift rather than just a quick snack.

Learn more

Specifications of OSQ TABOX 440

Volume

440 ml

Dimensions

105 x 105 x 40 mm

Temperature range

-30°C to +70°C

What makes TABOX 440 convenient

The new format retains the key advantages of TABOX PRO: quick assembly, stackability, excellent product display and reliable performance when exposed to moisture, grease, and temperature changes.

Wide display window

The product is fully visible, making it easier for customers to assess the filling, shape, decoration, and freshness before opening.

Moisture and grease resistance

The barrier layer and sealed bottom help the packaging maintain its shape and appearance when in contact with sauces, creams, oils, and juicy ingredients.

Suitable for hot, cold, and frozen foods

The temperature range from -30°C to +70°C makes the packaging suitable for display, delivery, refrigeration, and shock freezing.

Quick assembly

The packaging is quickly formed and works well for businesses where assembly speed, stable shape, and neat presentation are essential.

For whom & for what purposes

OSQ TABOX 440 is designed for attractive single-item packaging without compromising compact size or product protection. It is ideal for products that need to be presented neatly, emphasize their value, and remain protected during takeaway, delivery, or shelf display.

Confectionery and bakeries

For mousse desserts, cupcakes, donuts, macarons, and small sweet combos.

Sushi, rolls, and Asian cuisine

For small sushi or roll sets, snacks, and dishes where product visibility is important.

Ready meals, retail, and take-away

For single-portion shelf display, kids' menu, tasting portions and non-food items.

A new size in the TABOX PRO range

The range already includes TABOX PRO 300 and TABOX PRO 500. TABOX 440 takes the middle position: more compact than the rectangular format, yet offering more space than the smallest option and particularly well suited for square product placement.
