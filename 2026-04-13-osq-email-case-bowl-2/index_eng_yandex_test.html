What makes OSQ CASE BOWL® convenient
CASE BOWL® is not just a new container, but a fresh approach towards serving ready-made meals. OSQ Group was the first to introduce the format CASE BOWL® in Russia and the CIS, continuing the development of the legendary BOWL LINE. The construction made of virgin fiberboard with a barrier coating performs reliably with both hot and cold dishes.
Reliable stacking - ideal for display and delivery
Ready for sealing - compatible with film, lidding foil, and paper for production needs
Ready to use - no assembly or extra steps before using
0% glue - thoughtfully designed composition without added adhesives
Microwave safe - meals can be reheated directly in the packaging
Freezer safe - maintains shape and performance at low temperatures
